About Me

I am a final year student of the Informatics Engineering Study Program at Universitas Perjuangan Tasikmalaya with a great interest in web development and technology. During my studies, I have been actively involved in various web application development projects, both using native technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as modern frameworks like Laravel and React. Currently, I am developing a web-based decision support system using fuzzy logic, which aims to provide more accurate recommendations based on certain parameters.
This project reflects my interest in applying informatics theory to solve real-world problems. Additionally, I have a high curiosity about the latest technological developments, good analytical skills to identify effective and efficient solutions, and expertise in working collaboratively in teams. I also quickly adapt to new technologies and always strive to improve work efficiency through innovation.
